Interactive Message Board

Type a message and watch it appear randomly on the screen! Click on messages to remove them.

How It Works

  • Type your message in the input box above
  • Click "Post" or press Enter to submit
  • Your message will appear randomly on the screen
  • Messages appear with random colors, sizes, and rotations
  • Click any message to remove it from the screen
  • Try dragging messages around after you post them!
